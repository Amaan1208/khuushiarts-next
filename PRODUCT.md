# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Art collectors and buyers of traditional Indian art (Tanjore, Miniature, Pichwai, Canvas). They arrive to browse Khuushi's collection, evaluate the quality and provenance of individual pieces, and either purchase existing work or commission custom pieces through the contact page. Secondary audience: friends, family, and social-media visitors exploring the floating 3D gallery.

## Product Purpose

KhuushiArts is the online gallery of a single artist, Khuushi. It presents her body of traditional Indian artwork with museum-grade seriousness: an interactive 3D "floating gallery" as the front door, a curated collection organized by art form, an editorial about page telling the heritage story, and a bespoke commission-intake page that converts interest into custom art requests. Success = visitors linger in the gallery, understand the art forms, and submit commission requests.

## Brand Personality

Heritage, reverent, luxurious. The site treats traditional Indian art forms with the gravity of a museum retrospective and the polish of a high-end gallery. Voice is quiet, knowledgeable, and unhurried; the art is always the loudest thing on the page.

## Anti-references

- Etsy/marketplace energy: no price badges, carts, discount banners, or grid-of-thumbnails commerce patterns.
- Generic SaaS landing-page grammar: no hero metrics, gradient text, or feature-card grids.
- Loud portfolio templates: no parallax overload, autoplaying carousels, or aggressive scroll-jacking.

## Design Principles

1. **The art is the interface.** Layout, motion, and color exist to frame the paintings; nothing competes with them.
2. **Museum pacing.** Generous whitespace, slow deliberate motion, one focal point at a time.
3. **Heritage told, not decorated.** Cultural context is conveyed through real editorial copy and typography, not ornamental clichés.
4. **Quiet luxury.** Restraint over spectacle: hairline rules, tactile paper texture, deep charcoal ink on cream.
5. **Every visitor can reach the commission form.** The path from admiring a piece to requesting one is never more than a click away.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Sensible defaults: WCAG AA contrast for all text (deep charcoal #2C2520 on cream passes), full keyboard navigability for nav/forms, visible focus states, and `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all animations (crossfade or instant in place of movement).
